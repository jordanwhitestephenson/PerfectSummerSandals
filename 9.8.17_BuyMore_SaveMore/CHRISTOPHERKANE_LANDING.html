Take A Walk On The Wild Side

On September 19, 2016 renowned designer Christopher Kane surprised guests at his London Fashion Week show by unveiling his latest vision: high fashion Crocs, featuring marbled designs and embellished with rough mineral charms. This unexpected collaboration generated worldwide interest, resulting in press coverage in publications such as Vogue, Elle and Cosmopolitan. For 2017, the Christopher Kane x Crocs collaboration offers a new vision in footwear via four unique colorways. Limited quantities, and proudly not for everyone.

Who is Christopher Kane?

Well known in the fashion world for his highly creative and playful signature of mixing innovation with rebellious femininity, Christopher Kane has been the recipient of many industry accolades and awards, including four British Fashion Awards - winning Designer of the Year in 2013.
